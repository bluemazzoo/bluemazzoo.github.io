Copyright Links

  • Copyright.gov - This links to Title 17, which includes all US Copyright Law (e.g. Ch.1 covers Subject Matter and Scope of Copyright).
  • EFF Explanation of the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) - A short article explaining the DMCA.
  • Stanford: The Center for Internet and Society - this website contains lots of resources about copyright law and fair use.
  • Technology and Marketing Law Blog - this is Eric Goldman's blog about all things internet law. It includes infromation about the most recent case law and trends in internet law, specifically the DMCA and CDA 230.
